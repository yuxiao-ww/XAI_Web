Explainable AI

Explainable AI (XAI) refers to artificial intelligence systems that are designed to be transparent and clear in their decision-making processes and outcomes. The goal of XAI is to provide sufficient information to allow human users to understand and trust the decisions made by AI systems. XAI is particularly important in high-stakes fields like healthcare, finance, and law enforcement, where understanding the basis of AI decisions is crucial for ethical and practical reasons. By making AI systems more interpretable, XAI aims to bridge the gap between human decision-making and AI, ensuring that AI technology can be used responsibly and effectively.
